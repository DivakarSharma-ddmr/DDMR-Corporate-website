Test Smarter. Plan Better. Act Faster.

In fast-moving markets, reacting isn’t enough — you need to anticipate. Modeliq is DataDiggers’ simulation and modeling engine, built for decision-makers who want to see potential outcomes before they commit resources.

By combining advanced statistical logic with AI-generated synthetic data, Modeliq creates a trusted sandbox for:

Testing “what-if” scenarios

Simulating real-world market conditions

Forecasting with precision and agility

Whether you’re a research agency optimizing client strategies or a brand exploring new product launches, Modeliq helps you plan without guesswork.

Why Choose

Modeliq?

Traditional research can take weeks. By the time you have results, the opportunity may have shifted.

Modeliq allows you to:

Simulate market behavior instantly

Explore consumer reactions without fielding delays

Adjust strategies in real time to stay ahead of the curve

Industry data shows that companies using predictive modeling are 2.9× more likely to achieve revenue growth above industry averages (Deloitte).

Key Advantages

Scenario Testing Made Simple

Test multiple strategic conditions — from pricing shifts to marketing spend reallocation — and see potential outcomes before making high-stakes decisions.

Data You Can Trust

Modeliq simulations are grounded in validated market patterns and behavior-driven logic, ensuring output is realistic and relevant — not just statistical noise.

Speed and Flexibility

Run complex simulations in hours, enabling agile iterations without derailing current timelines.

Cost-Effective Forecasting

Break the dependency on constant new data collection. Modeliq lets you run more scenarios at a lower cost.

Privacy-Safe Exploration

Because Modeliq uses synthetic data, there’s zero risk of exposing personal or proprietary information — perfect for confidential modeling and early-stage ideation.
