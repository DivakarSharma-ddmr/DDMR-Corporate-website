Company Overview

Founded in 2015, DataDiggers is a global market research agency focused on speed, precision, and data integrity. Our solutions streamline research through advanced automation, AI guidance, and deeply profiled proprietary panels.

We deliver:

Milestones

From vision to global impact.

2015 – 2017

Founded in Bucharest and quickly expanded across Europe, gaining global clients and debuting at London's Insight Show.

2018 – 2020

Grew proprietary panels worldwide, opened hubs in India, Canada and Singapore, and became a full-service company.

2021 – 2022

Earned Quirk's Top Company recognition, joined SORMA, and presented at ESOMAR and Succeet22.

2023 – 2024

Expanded presence to New York, presented and were Title Sponsors at MRMW Singapore. Launched the Brainactive platform.

2025

Gold Sponsor at IIeX Bangkok & Amsterdam, and speaker at ESOMAR Connect Bucharest.
