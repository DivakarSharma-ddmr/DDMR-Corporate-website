NEOPULSE

NeoPulse is DataDiggers’ quarterly barometer that measures public and B2B sentiment toward the world’s most transformative innovations — starting with the U.S. market.

We go beyond headlines to deliver credible, recurring insights into how consumers and professionals actually adopt, use, and perceive emerging technologies such as AI, robotics, VR/AR, 3D printing, bio-integrated tech, next-gen fuels, and more.

Whether you’re a market research agency, innovation team, or public institution, NeoPulse offers data you can act on, enabling smarter, future-ready decisions in a rapidly shifting landscape.

Why NeoPulse?

In an era when tech hype often overshadows reality, NeoPulse focuses on facts. Each wave equips you to:

Track actual adoption, not just awareness

Understand barriers, sentiment, and trust by demographic

Compare consumer vs. B2B adoption patterns

Spot gaps, resistance, and market openings in time to respond

Benchmark changes quarter over quarter

We deliver insights built on speed, scale, and rigor, so you’re not just informed — you’re prepared.

What You Get

Verified U.S. data from 1,000+ respondents (including B2B).

Key metrics on awareness, sentiment, adoption, and resistance.

Demographic and behavioral crossbreaks.

Optional raw data and enterprise-grade deliverables.

Slide-ready outputs backed by DataDiggers’ trusted methodology.

Built on Quality You Can Trust

Data quality is non-negotiable. NeoPulse is powered by MyVoice, our proprietary global panel of over 2 million profiled, verified respondents. Every single response passes multiple quality checks, including:

GeoIP location verification

Digital fingerprinting

IPQS fraud detection

CAPTCHA & deduplication

AI-assisted quality control & open-end validation

Learn more about our approach to data quality

Each wave is overseen by a dedicated Research Director, supported by experienced project managers and reviewed by our in-house analysis and quality teams.
