Accuracy Starts with Authenticity

Quality isn’t optional — it’s foundational. Every panelist in our system goes through rigorous validation, including:

Double Opt-In: Ensures willing, informed participation

GeoIP Verification: Confirms geographic accuracy

Digital Fingerprinting: Detects duplicates and suspicious activity

AI-Powered Fraud Detection: Flags bots, inattentive users, and fake responses

Automatic Deduplication: Prevents multiple entries from the same user

We also use IPQS, one of the most advanced validation layers in the market research industry, ensuring that every response is genuine, consistent, and usable.
